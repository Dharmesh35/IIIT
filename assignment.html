More About IIIT KOTA

IIIT Kota (IIITK) is one of the youngest IIITs in the country that was established in the year 2013 under the Public-Private-Partnership (PPP) model. The institute is recognized as an institute of national importance. It is a joint venture of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) Government of India and the state government (Rajasthan).

At present, IIIT Kota is offering UG, PG, Ph.D. level courses in the field of Engineering and Technology. B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is the flagship course of IIIT Kota. Admissions to programme are offered on the basis of scores in JEE Main, followed by JoSAA Counselling. The JEE Main cutoff for CSE at IIIT Kota ranges from 5342-17897 for the general category. From the placement perspective, IIIT Kota has been providing impressive placement opportunities to its students. In the recent placement drive of 2021, the institute achieved a 94.87% placement rate. The highest salary package offered was INR 43 LPA mark, while the average package was INR 9 LPA. Adobe, Amazon, Deloitte, Goldman Sachs, Infosys, HSBC, L&T, Nykaa, and Zomato are some of the visiting companies at IIIT Kota.
